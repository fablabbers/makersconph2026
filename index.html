01 — ABOUT

About MakersCon PH

MakersCon PH is an annual gathering of makers, innovators, educators, hobbyists, and FABLABs from across the Philippines to share ideas, projects, and collaborations. This year’s convention is hosted by the UP CFA FABLAB as it celebrates 10 years of making, learning, and community-building.

About the UP CFA FabLab

The UP CFA FabLab is a learning and prototyping space that provides access to digital fabrication and creative technologies for students, communities, MSMEs, and emerging makers.
Guided by art and design principles, the lab supports creative experimentation, collaboration, and impact-driven making through both traditional and digital fabrication.
As a founding member of FabLabs PH and part of the global FabLab network, the UP CFA FabLab continues to connect local makers to a wider international community.
